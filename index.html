Canberra’s Art Biennial: contour 556 is an equal access event, entirely free to the public.

A diverse group of artists presents diverse stories to Canberra’s multicultural community. contour 556 provides opportunities for audience engagement through a series of public programs including performances, talks, tours, workshops, a people’s choice award and digital platform.

Public. Art. Matters.

The artworks showcased at successive contour556 biennials are the opposite of the congealed celebrations of imperial triumphalism which still form the bulk of sculpture punctuating public spaces in the west. Instead they are open, intriguing, nuanced invitations to engage with contemporary artists’ interrogation of life as we know it and live it.
